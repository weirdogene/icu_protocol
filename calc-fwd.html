Free Water Deficit in Hypernatremia
Estimates volume of electrolyte-free water needed to correct hypernatremia. Correct at ≤ 10–12 mEq/L per 24 h to avoid cerebral edema.
01 Inputs
02 Formula
TBW = weight × fraction (0.45–0.6) FWD = TBW × [(current Na / target Na) − 1] Rate ≤ 10–12 mEq/L per 24 h

Adrogué HJ, Madias NE. Hypernatremia. N Engl J Med. 2000;342(20):1493-9.
