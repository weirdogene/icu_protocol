FENa
Fractional Excretion of Sodium
Differentiates prerenal AKI from intrinsic tubular injury. Unreliable in CKD, after diuretics, or in contrast nephropathy / glomerulonephritis.
01 Inputs
02 Formula
FENa (%) = (UNa × PCr) / (PNa × UCr) × 100
03 Interpretation
< 1%Prerenal AKI (volume depletion, hepatorenal, CHF)
1–2%Indeterminate
> 2%Intrinsic AKI / ATN

Espinel CH. The FENa test. Use in the differential diagnosis of acute renal failure. JAMA. 1976;236(6):579-81.
