Anion Gap (AG)
Estimates unmeasured anions for the differential of metabolic acidosis. Provide albumin for correction when < 4.0 g/dL.
01 Inputs
02 Formula
AG = Na − (Cl + HCO₃) [normal 8–12] Corrected AG = AG + 2.5 × (4.0 − albumin)
03 Interpretation
< 8Low AG — hypoalbuminemia, paraproteinemia
8–12Normal
> 12High AG acidosis (MUDPILES / GOLDMARK)

Kraut JA, Madias NE. Serum anion gap: its uses and limitations in clinical medicine. Clin J Am Soc Nephrol. 2007;2(1):162-74.
