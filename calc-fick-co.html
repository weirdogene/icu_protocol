Cardiac Output (Fick)
Fick's Principle — Cardiac Output
Estimates cardiac output from oxygen consumption and arteriovenous oxygen content difference. Requires true mixed venous sample (PA catheter) for accuracy.
01 Inputs
02 Formula
CO (L/min) = VO₂ / [1.34 × Hgb × (SaO₂ − SvO₂) × 10] CI (L/min/m²) = CO / BSA Estimated VO₂ ≈ 125 × BSA

Fick A. Über die Messung des Blutquantums in den Herzventrikeln. Sitzber Physik Med Ges Würzburg. 1870;2:16.
