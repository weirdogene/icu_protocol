Na Correction (Hyperglycemia)
Corrected Sodium in Hyperglycemia
Estimates true serum sodium when hyperglycemia causes osmotic water shift into the vascular space, producing pseudohyponatremia. Hillier factor (2.4) is more accurate at glucose > 400 mg/dL.
01 Inputs
02 Formula
Katz: Corrected Na = Na + 1.6 × (Glucose − 100) / 100 Hillier: Corrected Na = Na + 2.4 × (Glucose − 100) / 100

Katz MA. Hyperglycemia-induced hyponatremia—calculation of expected serum sodium depression. N Engl J Med. 1973;289(16):843-4.

Hillier TA, Abbott RD, Barrett EJ. Hyponatremia: evaluating the correction factor for hyperglycemia. Am J Med. 1999;106(4):399-403.
