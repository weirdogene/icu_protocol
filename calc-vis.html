VIS
Vasoactive-Inotropic Score
Quantifies hemodynamic support intensity. Originally validated in postoperative pediatric cardiac patients; higher scores correlate with worse outcomes.
01 Current infusion doses
02 Formula
VIS = dopamine + dobutamine + 100 × epinephrine + 100 × norepinephrine + 10 × milrinone + 10,000 × vasopressin

Gaies MG, Gurney JG, Yen AH, et al. Vasoactive-inotropic score as a predictor of morbidity and mortality in infants after cardiopulmonary bypass. Pediatr Crit Care Med. 2010;11(2):234-8.
