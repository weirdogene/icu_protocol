Creatinine Clearance
Cockcroft-Gault Equation
Estimated creatinine clearance for renal dosing. Not validated in acute kidney injury, pregnancy, or extremes of body composition.

02 Formula
CrCl = ((140 − age) × weight) / (72 × sCr) × 0.85 (if female)
03 Interpretation (KDIGO stage)
≥ 90Normal / G1
60–89Mildly decreased / G2
30–59Moderate / G3
15–29Severe / G4
< 15Kidney failure / G5

Cockcroft DW, Gault MH. Prediction of creatinine clearance from serum creatinine. Nephron. 1976;16(1):31-41.
