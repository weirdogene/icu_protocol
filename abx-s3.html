항생제 잠금 요법Antibiotic Lock Therapy

1.일반 원칙

(1)적응증
•항생제 잠금요법(Antibiotic lock therapy, ALT)은 중심정맥관을 제거하기 어려운 카테터 관련 혈류감염(catheter-related bloodstream infection, CRBSI) 환자에게 적용
•ALT 단독 요법으로 사용해서는 안 되며, 반드시 전신 항생제 치료와 병용
(2)시행 방법
•희석: 항생제는 0.9% 생리식염수 또는 5% 포도당 수액에 희석
•주입
–각 도관에 3 mL씩 하루 2회 투여
–투여 전 중심정맥관으로부터 잔류 용액 3 mL를 제거한 뒤, 새로 조제한 항생제 잠금 용액을 주입
(3)금기증
•항생제 또는 헤파린에 알레르기 / 과민반응이 있는 환자

2.약제별 잠금 용액 조제

Vancomycin XVACP5 mg/mL + heparin 50 IU/mL
① Vancomycin 0.5 g/vial (XVACP) + 0.9% NS 49 mL 혼합 (총량 50 mL, 최종 농도 vancomycin 10 mg/mL)
② ①에서 만든 용액 1.5 mL를 취함
③ Heparin 1,000 IU/10 mL (XHEPA10)에서 1.5 mL를 취함 (= heparin 150 IU)
④ ②와 ③ 용액 혼합 (총량 3 mL)
Ciprofloxacin XCIPR22 mg/mL
• Ciprofloxacin 200 mg/100 mL premix bag (XCIPR2)에서 3 mL를 직접 취함
Gentamicin XGM2 mg/mL + heparin 50 IU/mL
① Gentamicin 80 mg/2 mL/amp (XGM) 1 mL + 0.9% NS 9 mL 혼합 (총량 10 mL, 최종 농도 gentamicin 4 mg/mL)
② ①에서 만든 용액 1.5 mL를 취함
③ Heparin 1,000 IU/10 mL (XHEPA10)에서 1.5 mL를 취함 (= heparin 150 IU)
④ ②와 ③ 용액 혼합 (총량 3 mL)
Amikacin XAMK2.5 mg/mL
① Amikacin 250 mg/2 mL/vial (XAMK) 1 mL + 0.9% NS 49 mL 혼합 (총량 50 mL, 최종 농도 amikacin 2.5 mg/mL)
② ①에서 만든 용액 3 mL를 취함

1.General Principles

(1)Indication
•Antibiotic lock therapy (ALT) is indicated for patients with catheter-related bloodstream infection (CRBSI) in whom removal of the central venous catheter is not feasible
•ALT should not be used as monotherapy and must be administered in combination with systemic antibiotic therapy
(2)Procedure
•Dilution: Antibiotics should be diluted in 0.9% normal saline or 5% dextrose in water
•Instillation
–Instill 3 mL into each lumen, twice daily
–Prior to instillation, withdraw 3 mL of any residual solution from the central venous catheter, then instill the newly prepared antibiotic lock solution
(3)Contraindication
•Patients with known allergy or hypersensitivity to the antibiotic or heparin

2.Lock-solution Preparation

Vancomycin XVACP5 mg/mL + heparin 50 IU/mL
① Mix vancomycin 0.5 g/vial (XVACP) + 0.9% NS 49 mL (total volume: 50 mL, final concentration: vancomycin 10 mg/mL)
② Withdraw 1.5 mL from solution ①
③ Withdraw 1.5 mL from heparin 1,000 IU/10 mL (XHEPA10) (= heparin 150 IU)
④ Mix solutions ② + ③ (total volume: 3 mL)
Ciprofloxacin XCIPR22 mg/mL
• Withdraw 3 mL directly from ciprofloxacin 200 mg/100 mL premix bag (XCIPR2)
Gentamicin XGM2 mg/mL + heparin 50 IU/mL
① Mix gentamicin 80 mg/2 mL/amp (XGM) 1 mL + 0.9% NS 9 mL (total volume: 10 mL, final concentration: gentamicin 4 mg/mL)
② Withdraw 1.5 mL from solution ①
③ Withdraw 1.5 mL from heparin 1,000 IU/10 mL (XHEPA10) (= heparin 150 IU)
④ Mix solutions ② + ③ (total volume: 3 mL)
Amikacin XAMK2.5 mg/mL
① Mix amikacin 250 mg/2 mL/vial (XAMK) 1 mL + 0.9% NS 49 mL (total volume: 50 mL, final concentration: amikacin 2.5 mg/mL)
② Withdraw 3 mL from solution ①
