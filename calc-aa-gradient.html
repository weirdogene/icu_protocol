A-a Gradient
Alveolar-arterial Oxygen Gradient
Differentiates hypoxemia mechanisms: normal gradient suggests hypoventilation or low FiO₂; widened gradient suggests V/Q mismatch, shunt, or diffusion impairment.

02 Formula
PAO₂ = FiO₂ × (Patm − 47) − PaCO₂ / 0.8 A-a gradient = PAO₂ − PaO₂ Expected (age-adjusted) ≈ age/4 + 4
03 Interpretation
NormalHypoventilation or low FiO₂
WidenedV/Q mismatch, shunt, diffusion impairment
