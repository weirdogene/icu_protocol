Corrected Calcium
Albumin-Corrected Serum Calcium
Adjusts measured total calcium for hypoalbuminemia. Ionized Ca is preferred when available (especially in critical illness).
01 Inputs
02 Formula
Corrected Ca = measured Ca + 0.8 × (4.0 − albumin)
03 Reference Range
< 8.5Hypocalcemia
8.5–10.5Normal
> 10.5Hypercalcemia

Payne RB, Little AJ, Williams RB, Milner JR. Interpretation of serum calcium in patients with abnormal serum proteins. Br Med J. 1973;4(5893):643-6.
