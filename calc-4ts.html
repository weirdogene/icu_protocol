헤파린 유발 혈소판 감소증 (HIT) 임상 평가Pretest Clinical Score for HIT
헤파린 유발 혈소판 감소증(HIT)의 임상 확률을 평가하는 4가지 항목의 합산 점수. 각 항목 0–2점, 총 0–8점.Scores clinical probability of heparin-induced thrombocytopenia. Four categories, each 0–2 points (total 0–8).
01 혈소판 감소증Thrombocytopenia
02 혈소판 감소 시점Timing of Platelet Count Fall
03 혈전증 또는 기타 후유증Thrombosis or Other Sequelae
04 혈소판 감소의 다른 원인Other Causes of Thrombocytopenia
05 결과 해석Interpretation
0–3저위험 (HIT 확률 낮음, < 5%)Low probability (< 5%)
4–5중등도 위험 (~14%)Intermediate probability (~14%)
6–8고위험 (~64%)High probability (~64%)

Lo GK, Juhl D, Warkentin TE, et al. Evaluation of pretest clinical score (4 T's) for the diagnosis of heparin-induced thrombocytopenia. J Thromb Haemost. 2006;4(4):759-65.
